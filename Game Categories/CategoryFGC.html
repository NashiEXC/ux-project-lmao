What are fighting games?


The fighting game genre involves combat between characters, often one-on-one battles. The mechanics of combat often features blocking, grappling, counter-attacking, and the ability to chain attacks together into "combos". Battles are usually set in a fixed-size arena along a two-dimensional plane, where characters navigate horizontally by walking or dashing, and vertically by jumping. -Adapted from Wikipedia


FGC in esports

Most view fighting games as one of the most prestigious genres offered in esports. Usually due to individual nature of competition and the symetrical design of most fighting games, it is seen as the purest form of skill versus skill in esports
