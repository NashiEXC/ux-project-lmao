What are fighting games?


The fighting game genre involves combat between characters, often one-on-one battles. The mechanics of combat often features blocking, grappling, counter-attacking, and the ability to chain attacks together into "combos". Battles are usually set in a fixed-size arena along a two-dimensional plane, where characters navigate horizontally by walking or dashing, and vertically by jumping. -Adapted from Wikipedia


FGC in esports

Most view fighting games as one of the most prestigious genres offered in esports. Usually due to individual nature of competition and the symetrical design of most fighting games, it is seen as the purest form of skill versus skill in esports

GAMES THAT WE PLAY

Street Fighter 6

Street Fighter 6 is a methodical and highly technical Fighting Game that requires a good understanding of mechanics, matchup knowledge and execution to thrive. That said, it is far from slow as one good play can shift the momentum of the match drastically along with the fact that several characters thrive by creating frantic situations. All of this is complemented by the Drive System, designed to encourage player creativity. The perfect game for patient and analytical players.

GUILTY GEAR -STRIVE-

GUILTY GEAR -STRIVE- is a breakneck Anime-style Fighting Game that draws heavy inspiration from Metal and Rock. Its comparatively simpler mechanics coupled with its breakneck pace makes each match an exchange of a thousand blows, forcing players to manage their opponent's lightning fast attacks and not crack under the pressure, all the while returning with their own barrage of attacks. Do not be fooled by it's simpler mechanics however, as each system is meticulously designed to be easily used yet all have surprising depth for those who spend their time to understand them. Perfect for players who thrive in fast-paced and high pressure situations.

Under Night In-birth II

Under Night In-birth II is a breakneck yet extremely technical Anime-style Fighting Game that requires deep knowledge of its systems and character matchups along with technical mastery in order to thrive. every character is drastically different compared to one another, requiring you to at the very least understand every member of the roster at a basic level. its breakneck place along with its complex and meticulously crafted combat systems also require you to be able to pull off techincally challenging inputs all the while managing you and your opponent's relentless barrage of blows. Perfect for players who are willing to push their limits.
