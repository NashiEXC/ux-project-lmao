FPS games, a brief introduction


A first-person shooter (FPS) is a video game usually centered on gun fighting seen from a first-person perspective, with the player experiencing the action directly through the eyes of the main character.
-Adapted from Wikipedia


FPS in esports

With the introduction and quick rise of multiplayer gaming in the fps genre, dedicated communities were formed that seeked out to refine and push their skills to the absolute limit. Hence the earliest form of comeptitive fps was born.
Nowadays, there is a dedicated subgenre of FPS games designed to be competitively played known as

Tactical FPS
